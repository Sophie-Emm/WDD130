About Me

I'm 21 and attending BYU-Idaho, majoring in cybersecurity. My favorite color is green, and I enjoy spending time outside. I love snowboarding and have recently started learning how to longboard.

Gear Purchasing for Starters

Small gear shops work perfectly for starters, or even a nice used board can be a great option. If you're serious about this, I recommend Nidecker for high-quality equipment.

Burton makes great coats and snow pants, but I wouldn't buy their boards—they tend to break easily.

The Places I've Been to in Idaho

Pomerelle

Located near Declo/Albion, this was my first mountain! It's great for beginners and offers night skiing.

Visit Pomerelle

Grand Targhee

Located in Alta, Wyoming, it’s a great resort for practicing skills with plenty of fresh snow.

Visit Grand Targhee

Schweitzer

Located in Sandpoint, ID, this is one of the best mountains I've been to. It has about 10 lifts and 92 trails.

Visit Schweitzer
